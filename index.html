Who We Are

Bengaluru's Electrical Reliability Experts

Dharthi Engineers is a Bengaluru-based electrical engineering services company specializing in maintenance, testing, commissioning, diagnostics, and reliability solutions for LT and HT electrical systems.

  • Specialize in improving equipment reliability, reducing downtime, and enhancing safety
